About

Research

ARMIS: Autonomous Mobile Sensing System for Indoor Environmental Monitoring

Indoor Air 2026 · Oral · 2025 – 2026

Built an autonomous mobile robot integrating IEQ sensors with multimodal vision. Applied Gaussian Process Regression for high-resolution environmental field reconstruction. Fused YOLOv5 with GPT-4o / GLM-4v VLMs for occupant characterization and thermal comfort assessment. Validated across 7,700 m² at Tsinghua. First / Co-first Author.

ArchEGraph: Geometry–Topology–Physics Aligned Building Energy Graph Dataset

NeurIPS 2026 · 2025 – Present

Constructed a benchmark of 5,481 buildings (49,326 cases) as heterogeneous graphs aligning geometry, topology, meteorology, and thermal loads. Defined M2G and G2E tasks with cross-building, cross-climate generalization. Tsinghua × UC Berkeley × MIT. Co-first Author (Equal).
